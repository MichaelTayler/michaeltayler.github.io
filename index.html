About Me

We are a physics and chemistry team exploring atomic- and nuclear-spin-based sensing technologies at ICFO in Barcelona, Spain. The institute is located about 5 minutes' walk from the sandy Mediterranean coast of Castelldefels. Our work combines theoretical models, experiment design, and open-source tools.
