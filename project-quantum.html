Quantum Control

This project explores coherent averaging, pulse sequence symmetry, and decoherence suppression in driven quantum systems.

Through simulation and analysis, we've developed robust pulse sequences that leverage polyhedral symmetries to enhance error resilience. These methods are relevant for NMR, ESR, and quantum computing applications.

The project involves both theoretical work — including Wigner matrix averaging — and practical implementations on spin-½ systems using composite pulses.
